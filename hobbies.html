Favourite Books

The Shadowhunter Chronicles

A popular fantasy book series written by Cassandra Clare, which is set in a world where humans coexist with supernatural beings such as demons, vampires, werewolves, and warlocks. This series has me in a chokehold, as I have read every story at least twice.

Dune

Dune is a classic science fiction novel written by Frank Herbert, and is considered one of the greatest science fiction works of all time. Although I initially found it a difficult read, after revisiting the story years later I finally appreciate the beauty of the narrative weaved together.

All the Light We Cannot See

A captivating story by Anthony Doerr about a blind French girl and German orphan with a talent for engineering. Set during World War II, the story had me feeling more emotional than I am willing to admit.

Favourite Movies and Shows

The Boys

A gripping superhero series that turns the conventional superhero narrative on its heads. I love the sharp satire, the intense action sequences, and especially the morally ambiguous characters. Of course, the gory visual effects are just the cherry on top!

Game of Throness

One of the most beloved fantasy series, the world of politics and supernatural elements had me greatly intrigued. Unfortunately, the final season's resolution left me greatly disappointed with the conclusion. Fortunately, the spin-off House of the Dragon has me right back up on the hype train.

The Originals

One of the rare times when the spin-off surpasses the original (Ha! Get it?). Joseph Morgan's portrayal of Klaus Mikaelson was incredible, making it hard not to empathize with him even during his darker deeds. The conclusion to the series was as emotional as it was beautiful.
